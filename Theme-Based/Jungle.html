Jungle

For this jungle-themed reception, the bride desired an Indo-Western look and the groom chose a Western tuxedo. To unify their styles, I designed the groom’s tuxedo with intricate eagle embroidery symbolizing strength and grace, while the bride’s silk lehenga featured matching light patchwork and delicate embroidery of the same bird motif. The coordinated details beautifully reflected their connection and the wild elegance of the theme.
